Our Founder

Hello!

Im Natalie Holloway – Founder and Head of Design here at Paper + Land. I studied the arts before spending the next 16 years heading up a successful design agency.

Paper + Land brings together my three greatest passions: Indian ink, contemporary design and the natural world.

I hope you enjoy browsing the collection. The pieces are designed to uplift the soul… I hope they do just that!

Natalie x
